\chapter{Methodology}\label{chap:Methodology}

Equation \eqref{eq:1} describes the kinematics model of the unicycle

\begin{equation} \label{eq:1}
\dot{q_i} = 
\begin{bmatrix}
	\dot{x_i}\\
	\dot{y_i}\\
	\dot{\varphi_i}
\end{bmatrix}
=
S_{1}(q_i)u_i
 \quad , \quad
  u_i = 
\begin{bmatrix}
	v_i\\
	\omega_i
\end{bmatrix}
 \quad and \quad
S_{1}(q_i) = 
\begin{bmatrix}
	\cos\varphi_i & 0\\
	\sin\varphi_i & 0\\
	0 & 1
\end{bmatrix}
\end{equation}
